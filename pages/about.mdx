# About XIAO DAO

Welcome to XIAO DAO, an innovative tech community focused on learning, mentorship, networking, and education. We're a vibrant community built around a Discord server, aiming to bridge the gap in the tech education landscape.

## Who We Are

At XIAO DAO, we're dedicated to fostering an inclusive platform where individuals can freely learn, share, and grow in their tech journey. We bring together tech enthusiasts, professionals, and novices, all united by a shared passion for technology.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a dynamic learning environment that combines structured learning, community interaction, and mentorship. We believe in open-source learning and the power of community collaboration.

## Our Community Guidelines

To ensure a safe, respectful, and productive learning experience for everyone, we've established community guidelines that all members are expected to adhere to. You can read our community guidelines [here](/community-guidelines).

## Join Us

Join us in creating a more inclusive and empowering future in tech education. Whether you're a tech newbie or a seasoned professional, we believe you can both contribute to and benefit from our community.

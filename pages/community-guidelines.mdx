# Community Guidelines

Welcome to XIAO DAO! Our community is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment where members can learn, share, and grow. To maintain the integrity of our community, we kindly ask all members to adhere to the following guidelines:

## 1. Respect

Treat every member with kindness and respect. Harassment, discrimination, and bullying of any kind are strictly prohibited.

## 2. No Financial Discussions

In line with our commitment to free and open-source learning, we do not allow discussions involving money or financial transactions within the community.

## 3. Stay On Topic

Please ensure your contributions are relevant to the topic of the channel or discussion. Off-topic discussions can detract from the learning experience.

## 4. No Spamming

Spamming disrupts the flow of conversation and can be frustrating to other members. Please refrain from posting repetitive or irrelevant messages.

## 5. Be Mindful of Others' Privacy

Respect the privacy of other members. Do not share others' personal information without their explicit consent.

## 6. Intellectual Property

When sharing content, make sure you have the right to do so. Respect copyright laws and always credit the original author.

Violations of these guidelines may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent banning from the community, depending on the severity of the violation. 

By joining and participating in the XIAO DAO community, you agree to abide by these guidelines. 

We look forward to a respectful, productive, and enriching exchange of ideas and learning in our community. 

If you have any questions or need to report a violation of these guidelines, please [contact us](https://discord.gg/HgMH9xJM).
